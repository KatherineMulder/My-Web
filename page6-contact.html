    About Ruby

    Ruby's holiday home is a warm cozy house a short ten-minute walk to Porirua city. A two-minute walk to takeaways and a park for kids to play also with netball and tennis courts. Have jerry Colin's rugby stadium five minutes -walk away.

    Porirua city contains a north city plaza with plenty of shops and movie theatres. Great variety of places to eat. There's Te Rauparaha Arena, which hosts international netball and domestic netball matches. Have an indoor swimming pool with adults, kids' pools, saunas, and spa pools.

    Only five minutes -walk to the railway station if you want to head into Wellington city or head up to Kapiti coast.
